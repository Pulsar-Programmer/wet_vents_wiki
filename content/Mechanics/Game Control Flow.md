
COMMANDS via [[SussyBot]] are the staple of this project as it allows a central server for the game to be run on.
This game is designed with the spirit of preventing as many abuses as possible, but if an abuse comes up, make sure to do the [[Report]] command.

Before a game can be started, players must join up with the [[Play]] command.
The Game starts with a [[Start]] command.
Rounds last as long as is necessary until someone wins.

Each person is given a role where they will have a certain **win condition**.
For the [[Crewmate|Crewmates]] to win, all tasks must be completed or all killing roles must be voted out or eliminated.
For the [[Imposter|Imposters]] to win, they must have killed all players.
Neutrals have their own goal specific to their role.
Crewmates win together and Imposters win together whereas typically Neutrals will earn solo wins, but not always.

Interactions during the school day are tracked using [[Tokens]]. All exchanged Tokens must be reported using [[Receive]] and [[Give]] by 4:00.

At 4:00, the events of the day are reported and everyone can see what has happened. 

If you are killed, you are turned into a ghost. A ghost may not leak information about the game to other players but get a dedicated space in the Discord and can also piece together the mystery or do `/role` to figure out the role of somebody. PLEASE don't ruin the game by leaking information as to who is what role. This is seen as **one of the only serious offenses** and can get you **banned** from future games.

After the day is up, at 7:00 to account for people who have athletic activities or other after-school commitments, the meeting time begins. It is designed to last at most 30 minutes with on average maybe about 10 minutes, but it can last for however long is necessary.
The aim of the meeting is to vote out a player or skip vote.

The days end at 10:00, where no more voting can be done and the results of the day are recorded.

That's all, have fun!!! :D
