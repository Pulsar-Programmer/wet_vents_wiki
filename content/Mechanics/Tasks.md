Tasks are implemented by finding certain signs around the building and filling the blanks verbatim with what they say.
To complete or view your tasks, you can use the [[Task]] interface.

## Example
For example, you can be given five tasks that hint to a location or have you fill in the blanks.
Like:
At GHS, we are \_\_\_, kind, and compassionate.
The task would be finding the sign and what goes in that location.

## Config
`task_amt: u8 = 7`: Amount of tasks Crewmates are given to complete.
