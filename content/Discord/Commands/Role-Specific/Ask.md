`ask [question] ?[target]`
The [[Medium]] may ask a dead player a question.
They are allowed to choose whether to ask someone in particular or ask all the ghosts.
The [[Medium]] may ask a question by choosing from a dropdown of acceptable questions.
Ghosts may respond by clicking a dropdown of acceptable answers.
Even if the question is broadly asked, the player ghost who answered the question is displayed in the personal announcement.
Note also that ghosts can lie.

## Questions and Answers List

**What role were you?**
Here the ghost may select from any role. 

**Where did you die?**
Here the ghost may select from many dropdowns:
WING: A, B, C, etc.
FLOOR: 1, 2
ROOM: 107, 109, etc.
or/and
NAME: Library, Gym, Hallway, etc.
or/and
IN-MEETING, OUT OF SCHOOL(custom name)

**Which role killed you?**
Here the ghost may select from any role, including "I'm not sure."

**How many tasks did you complete before you died?**
Here the ghost can type a number.

**Did anyone else witness your death?**
Yes / No / I'm not sure / It happened very privately

**Did you trust your killer before you died?**
Yes / No / I had no suspicions / I didn't know who my killer was


**Would you have won if you survived?** 
Definitely / Probably / Unlikely / No chance
