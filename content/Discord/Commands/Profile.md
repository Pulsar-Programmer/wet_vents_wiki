`profile [username|color|stats] ?[value]`
Profile allows you to edit/view your profile.
It is like a profile customization mini app. 
You change pfp (among us character), view stats, etc.

`profile stats` lets you see your profile formatted.

`profile username [value]` lets you change your username server-wide.

`profile color [value]` lets you change the color of your name in the server and profile of your little Among Us character that is shown.

Certain data of stats is stored like:
- how many games you've won/lost
