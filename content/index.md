MODIFIERS are special rules that apply to groups of players with certain roles and can be randomly assigned.
ROLES are randomly assigned goals to certain players tied to their win condition.

TLDR:
Wet Vents is an in-person Among Us with games lasting a week where after each day we meet on Discord and can determine the imposters or evil roles. Players are killed by showing a certain symbol and your goal is to survive as a crewmate, sabotage as an imposter, or chase an independent goal with as a neutral.
