The Engineer can reroll other people's tasks if they are having a rough time and also remove tasks from team mates as a counter to the [[Saboteur]]. The engineer can do these actions at max three times combined per game.
To reroll someone's task they can use the [[Reroll]] command,
while they are given an optional token to use in order to remove tasks from someone.
If the target is not a crewmate, this does nothing.
