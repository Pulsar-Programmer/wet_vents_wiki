The Snitch, through completing tasks, gains information about the players over time in three stages.

Stage 1: ($\frac{1}{3}$ of Tasks)
After reaching stage 1, typically after 2 tasks, the Snitch is given a list of players where at least one of which is neutral.

Stage 2: ($\frac{2}{3}$ of Tasks)
After reaching stage 2, typically after 4 tasks, the Snitch is given a list of players where at least one of which is a killer.

Stage 3: Final Task
After reaching the final stage by completing the final task, you are given the amount of letters in the username of an imposter. However, you are also revealed to the imposters that you are the Snitch.
