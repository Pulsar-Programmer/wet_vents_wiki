The Witch can Curse players by exchanging a code.
The Witch may only curse at most one player per day.

A cursed player dies after the most recent Group Meeting, at the end of the day/start of the next day unless the Witch isn't alive (voted out or died in meeting or beforehand).

**Config**
`cursed_count: u8 = 1`: Amount of players that can be cursed per day.
