**Absolutely, yeah!** This game is free to enter, free to play, and free to enjoy for everyone - it is not a mandatory alternative intended to take attention away from the game but to be a fun and safe game that everyone can enjoy in addition to all else!!


## Once I am killed, can I talk about it with active players?
Technically, nothing is preventing ghosts from discussing with active crew members, however, this is pretty much the only large bannable/kickable offense and is an **incredibly discouraged practice** as it sells the game and results in cheating.
If you want to discuss, however, don't hesitate to discuss in the exclusive [[ghosts]] channel! Not to mention all the ways you can help out through players like the [[Medium]]!!

## Can I give people fake cards with codes?
**Yes, absolutely!** This is what makes it sneaky! You can fake an interaction by faking a code and giving it to somebody. The code won't do anything, but it will trick the player! As long as the rules of the codes are not broken and everything is submitted, anything can be done!!

## How does a kill work? Can’t somebody lie?
Technically, yes, someone can lie, but that is with all things like water wars, too.
But ultimately the imposter must send a `/give` command in order to register the kill. If this is not done by the end of school (4:00), it’s not valid. Alternatively, the victim can report their death with the `/receive` command by the end of school. In any event, their death will be posted by the end of school, allowing us to meet.
If the imposter killed illegally without showing the target the code or reporting it, the kill is **not valid**.
